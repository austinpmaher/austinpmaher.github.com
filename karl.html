Karl Freburger passed away unexpectedly in his sleep on May 10, 2023. He was 66 years old and had been courageously battling a frontotemporal dementia diagnosis for the last several years. His obituary can be found here.

Karl and I were friends for years and the words I have at hand cannot do justice to my love for him or my sadness at his passing. My deepest condolences to Janet, Kurt and Elise and all his friends and family.

OOPSLA 1990

Karl and I worked on DEC Trellis for several years. In 1990 we were getting ready for our first release and the whole team went to Ottawa for OOPSLA 1990 to show off what we had. I think this picture was at a conference banquet.

Portland

I think we were in Portland, maybe for SIGNMOD 1989? Karl, Lydia Bennett and myself, taking a break, sitting in the sun.

Timberline Lodge, Mt Hood

After the conference Karl and I drove up the Columbia River Gorge, checking out the waterfalls. We stayed at the Timberline Lodge and it snowed overnight.
